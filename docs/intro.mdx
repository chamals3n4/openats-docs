# What is OpenATS

OpenATS is an open-source Applicant Tracking System (ATS) designed to help organizations manage and streamline their hiring process from a single platform.

Recruiting often involves multiple tools, spreadsheets, emails, and disconnected workflows. OpenATS brings these activities together into a unified hiring workspace, enabling teams to attract talent, manage candidates, collaborate on evaluations, and make hiring decisions more efficiently.

Whether you are a startup building your first hiring process, a growing company scaling recruitment, or an organization looking for a transparent and customizable hiring solution, OpenATS provides the flexibility needed to adapt to different recruitment workflows.

## Why OpenATS?

Many organizations rely on recruitment software that is difficult to customize and expensive to scale. OpenATS provides a flexible open-source alternative that gives teams full control over their hiring workflows.

Organizations can use OpenATS as a ready-to-use applicant tracking system or as the foundation for building their own internal hiring platform. With complete transparency, customization, and ownership, teams can adapt the platform to fit their unique recruitment needs instead of adapting their process to the software.

OpenATS provides:

- Full ownership through open-source software
- Flexible and customizable hiring workflows
- A foundation for building internal recruitment platforms
- Reduced dependence on proprietary ATS vendors
- Improved collaboration across hiring teams

## Core Capabilities

### Candidate Management

OpenATS provides a complete view of every candidate throughout their hiring journey. Recruiters and hiring managers can access candidate profiles, track activity, review applications, and maintain organized records in a single location.

### Hiring Process Management

Every organization hires differently. OpenATS allows teams to create and manage hiring processes that reflect their unique recruitment strategies, ensuring consistency while maintaining flexibility.

### Visual Hiring Pipelines

Monitor candidate progress through every stage of recruitment using intuitive pipeline views. Teams can quickly understand where candidates are in the process and identify opportunities to improve hiring efficiency.

### Structured Assessments

Evaluate candidates using standardized assessment criteria, interview feedback, and scorecards. This helps organizations make informed and consistent hiring decisions while reducing bias and improving transparency.

## Built for Collaboration

Hiring is a team effort. OpenATS enables recruiters, interviewers, hiring managers, and other stakeholders to work together throughout the recruitment lifecycle. Shared visibility and structured feedback help teams align on hiring decisions and improve candidate experiences.

## Open Source and Community Driven

OpenATS is built as an open-source project with a focus on transparency, extensibility, and community collaboration. Organizations can adapt the platform to their needs while contributors help shape the future of the project.

We believe recruitment software should be accessible, flexible, and continuously improved by the people who use it.

## Who is OpenATS For?

OpenATS is suitable for:

- Startups building their first recruitment process
- Growing companies scaling hiring operations
- Recruitment teams seeking a customizable ATS
- Organizations that value transparency and ownership
- Open-source enthusiasts and contributors

## Our Vision

Our vision is to make hiring more organized, collaborative, and accessible for teams of all sizes.

By combining candidate management, hiring workflows, visual pipelines, and structured evaluations into a single platform, OpenATS aims to simplify recruitment while empowering organizations to make better hiring decisions.
